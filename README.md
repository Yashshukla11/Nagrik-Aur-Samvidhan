Many citizens struggle to understand the legalities surrounding their rights and duties. This includes:

- **Limited Legal Awareness:** A significant portion of the population lacks basic knowledge about their constitutional rights.
- **Inaccessible Resources:** Many legal resources are not designed for children, women, and rural communities, resulting in a knowledge gap.
- **Complex Language:** The intricate language of the Indian Constitution creates barriers to understanding.
